Bringing Real Estate to the Blockchain.

What is Landshare?

Landshare is an investment platform coming soon to the Binance Smart Chain enabling anyone to earn yields based on the value of real estate assets. With a focus on accessibility, simplicity, and yield maximization, Landshare offers a value-driven model for real estate based investments on the blockchain.

Bringing Real Estate to the Blockchain

Our platform features the Property Vault, a stablecoin vault capped by the value of real estate assets. The Property Vault represents the simplest way to profit from rental prices and property appreciation - simply stake your stablecoins, harvest your rewards, and withdraw at any time.

Our Features

Hassle Free

Landshare offers a hassle free platform for investors to earn yields based on the value of real estate assets.

Property Vault

The Property Vault features a dual-reward system, paying stakers in both LAND tokens and stablecoins.

Earn Yields

Landshare offers yields based on regional rental prices, and the rewards accumulate in real time.
